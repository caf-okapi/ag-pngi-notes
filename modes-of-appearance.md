# Modes of Appearance

Structures may be associated with generativity directly, or with relations among structures.

Some structures may be associated with generativity without reference to other structures. Others may be associated with relations, whether internal to a structure or among multiple structures. These may be described as emergent.

These two modes do not introduce a hierarchy. Structures associated with relations are not less fundamental than those associated directly with generativity.

This framework does not assume that either mode is required or that either is privileged. Both are permitted under non-governance.
